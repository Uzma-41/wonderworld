Stay safe on your travels

Keep Copies of Documents

Store digital copies of passport, visa, and ID in your email and cloud. Carry a physical photocopy separately from originals.

Travel Insurance

Always buy travel insurance covering medical emergencies, trip cancellation, and lost luggage. It's worth every penny.

Share Your Itinerary

Share your travel plans and hotel details with a trusted friend or family member. Check in regularly.

Secure Your Valuables

Use hotel safes, money belts, and anti-theft bags. Avoid flashing expensive jewelry or gadgets in crowded areas.

Stay Hydrated

Carry a reusable water bottle and stick to bottled or filtered water in unfamiliar destinations. Avoid ice in drinks.

Emergency Numbers

Save local emergency numbers, embassy contacts, and your hotel address. Download offline maps before you leave.
